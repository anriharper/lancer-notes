    Aleksy

    An employee of the Smith-Shimano Corpro who worked on the construction of White Swan, contracted as the personal mechanic for Bethany Sinclair.

    Aleksy has known Bethany for a long time, considering her a second daughter. He is a middle-aged, heavyset man, who seems very unassuming. But his history with the SSC and the Sinclair Foundation as a designer and R&D specialist speak to his aptitude as a mechanic.
